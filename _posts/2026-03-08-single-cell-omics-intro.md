Single-cell omics technologies have revolutionized our understanding of cellular heterogeneity.

Traditional bulk sequencing methods provide an average signal across millions of cells, masking important cellular differences. Single-cell approaches reveal rare cell populations, cell differentiation trajectories, cell-cell communication, and gene regulatory networks.

Key technologies include scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, and spatial transcriptomics.
